SAN JUAN OFFICIALS

Hon. Francis M. Zamora

City Mayor
Hon. Francisco Javier M. Zamora took his oath of office as Mayor of San Juan City on June 30, 2019. It was a hard-won victory, having freed the city from the clutches of one family who had monopolized the leadership of the city for almost five decades.

Ronaldo M. Zamora

City Congressman
Ronaldo “Ronny” Bayan Zamora serves as the representative of the Lone District of San Juan City in the Philippine Congress. He is responsible for having elevated San Juan from a municipality into a first-class city, having sponsored the cityhood bill in the House of Representatives and worked for its approval. This became Republic Act No. 9388, the Charter of San Juan City, which lapsed into law on March 11, 2007.

Jose Warren P. Villa

City Vice Mayor
Jose Warren “Warren” P. Villa is the Vice Mayor of San Juan City and is the Presiding Officer of the San Juan City Council. He is also vice chairman of the City Anti-Drugs Abuse Council (CADAC).He is known for his strong faith and belief in God. He is also known for his dedication and passion to be of service to the city of San Juan.
